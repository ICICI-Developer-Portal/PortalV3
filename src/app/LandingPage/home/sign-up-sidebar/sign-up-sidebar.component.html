    From signing up with Developer Portal to going live in our production environment is a four-step process.

    The amount of time this will take depends on the APIs you are using, your business model and the time and resources you have available.

    Step 01 - SignUp

    To get access to our sandbox and start testing our APIs straight away you register at Developer Portal.

    Step 02 - Talk to us about how we can help your business

    Contact us or get in touch with the RM assigned to you at any point and learn how our APIs can be customized to your needs.

    Step 03 - Get On-Board with UAT
    • In this our development team will get in touch with you and help you develop the required APIs according to the specifications provided by you.
    • For that you will need to raise a UAT request and provide some basic details about the required project.
    • For each raised request in UAT, it will have a unique ID assigned to it which will allow you to keep a track of the whole UAT development process.
    • Post the whole UAT development process the status of the request would turn to complete and then we are ready to make your services live.
    • Production access – You will need to fill a similar form for final production access.
    Step 04 - Go Live

    You’re now ready to launch! Our production support team will be there to ensure the whole process is seamless.

    Step 01 - Enter you Organization name.
    Step 02 - Provide Basic Details about your organization.
    Step 03 - Provide first point of contact(POC) from your organization from business perspective.
    Step 04 - Provide SPOC Contact Number.
    Step 05 - Provide ICICI Bank contact RM for your selected product’s domain. Provided in the initial registration mail or you can add apidevteam@icicibank.com
    Step 06 - Select your domain and related APIs to that
    Step 06.1 - If your requirement needs a crossover of domains, please raise multiple onboarding request for each domain separately.
    Step 07 - Provide the IP of the service that would connect to ICICI bank. For security measures each IP from third party needs to be whitelisted for authentication
    Step 08 - Provide Callback URL- this acts as a failsafe check, if any request looses it’s connection in between the communication, on which ICICI bank will provide the information regarding a particular communication.
    Step 09 - Provide your public certificate which would be used for encryption/decryption of the data which would communicate between your organization’s service and ICICI Bank.
    Download ICICI Bank NDA and submit it to ICICI Bank RM
    Step 10 - Your request is assigned a Unique Request ID which will help you to trace the status of your request, while it is being assessed by our business and technical teams.
    Step 11 - You can view the status of all your onboarding requests raised by you
